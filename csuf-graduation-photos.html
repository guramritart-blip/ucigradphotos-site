Campus Locations

Best Photo Spots at CSUF

Cal State Fullerton has a range of scenic locations perfect for graduation portraits. Here are the four I recommend most for your session.

Titan Student Union

The Titan Student Union is the social hub of CSUF and a symbol of campus life. Its modern glass and steel exterior provides a sleek, contemporary backdrop for graduation portraits. The wide plaza in front of the building offers plenty of space for group shots, and the surrounding landscaping adds pops of color and texture to your photos.

Pollak Library

Pollak Library is the academic heart of Cal State Fullerton. The building's strong geometric lines and concrete facade create a bold, structured look in photos. The entrance area and surrounding walkways offer clean compositions, and the large open space in front of the library works well for both solo and group portraits.

McCarthy Hall

McCarthy Hall sits in one of the most picturesque areas of the CSUF campus. The building is surrounded by mature trees and well-maintained landscaping that provide natural frames and rich, green backgrounds. The shaded walkways near McCarthy Hall are perfect for photos on warmer days when direct sunlight can be harsh.

Titan Walk

Titan Walk is the main pedestrian pathway that runs through the core of campus. Lined with trees and dotted with benches and open spaces, it is ideal for walking shots and candid moments. The path creates natural leading lines in your photos, and the changing scenery along the route means every shot feels different from the last.
