Meet Your Photographer

Hey, I'm Guramrit Malhotra. UCI alum, class of 2020. I've been shooting graduation portraits for 7 years now, and I've had the privilege of capturing this milestone for hundreds of graduates across Southern California.

My style is clean and professional. I use off-camera lighting to make sure your photos look polished no matter the conditions. I know the best spots on campus, the best times to shoot, and how to make you feel comfortable in front of the camera.

Whether it's just you, a duo with your best friend, or a group session with family, I'm here to make your graduation photos something you'll be proud of for years to come.
