Graduation Photography FAQ

Everything you need to know about your graduation photo session.

All raw photos are sent via cloud within 24 hours of the shoot. Your edited selections (30 images) are delivered within 7 to 10 days.

I prefer groups of 4 to 5 people maximum. This allows me to dedicate adequate time to crafting individual portraits for each person while still getting great group shots.

Most times work well, but 3 to 5 PM tends to be the sweet spot. The light is golden, the shadows are soft, and the campus is generally less crowded.

I focus on a clean, professional look using off-camera lighting techniques. I use natural elements and campus architecture to create polished, timeless graduation portraits.
