USC Program

USC Marshall School of Business Graduation Photos

The USC Marshall School of Business is one of the top business schools in the world. Whether you are completing your undergraduate business degree, MBA, or specialized master's program, your graduation photos should match the caliber of your Marshall education.

Marshall graduates wear USC's cardinal-and-gold regalia, often with the Marshall school stole or MBA sash. The rich red tones photograph beautifully against the campus's Romanesque architecture, especially at Doheny Library and Bovard Auditorium where the warm brick and stone complement the regalia colors.

For business graduates, I recommend a session that captures both the prestige of your degree and the spirit of USC. Start with Tommy Trojan for the iconic mascot shot, move to Doheny Library for the grand academic backdrop, and finish at the Steps of Troy or Bovard for variety. If you want a quieter spot, Mudd Hall offers dramatic architecture without the crowds.

Group shots are popular among MBA cohorts. The Steps of Troy is ideal for large groups, and I can coordinate sessions that accommodate your classmates. I also offer duo and triple rates that make group sessions more affordable per person.
