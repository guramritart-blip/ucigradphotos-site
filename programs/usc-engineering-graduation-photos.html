USC Program

USC Viterbi School of Engineering Graduation Photos

The USC Viterbi School of Engineering is one of the most prestigious engineering programs in the country. Whether you are graduating with a degree in Computer Science, Aerospace, Biomedical, Electrical, or any other engineering discipline, your graduation photos should reflect the prestige and rigor of your Viterbi education.

USC engineering graduates wear the cardinal-and-gold regalia with the Viterbi school stole or sash. The distinctive red and gold colors photograph beautifully against USC's brick buildings and stone architecture. I recommend bringing all your honors cords, society pins, and any customized cap you have prepared.

For USC engineering graduates, I recommend a session route that includes Tommy Trojan (the essential USC shot), Bovard Auditorium (for its architectural symmetry), and Doheny Library (for the grand, academic feel). The Steps of Troy is also excellent for group shots with fellow engineering graduates.

I travel to the USC campus from Orange County regularly for graduation sessions. Travel is built into my USC pricing, so there are no surprise fees. Your session includes professional off-camera lighting, all raw photos in 24 hours, and edited selections in 7 to 10 days.
