UCI Program

The Paul Merage School of Business Graduation Photos

The Paul Merage School of Business at UCI is known for its modern glass-and-steel architecture and its strong MBA, undergraduate business, and specialized master's programs. Your graduation photos should match the polished, professional image you have built throughout your business education.

The Merage Business School building itself is one of the best graduation photo spots on campus. The sleek modern design, glass walls, and open courtyard create a sharp, professional backdrop that is especially fitting for business graduates. I always include shots at your school's building to personalize your gallery.

Business graduates at UCI typically wear the standard black cap and gown with a Merage school stole or MBA sash. If you have honors cords, a customized cap, or cultural regalia, bring them all. These personal touches make your photos uniquely yours.

I recommend combining the business school with the Infinity Fountain and Aldrich Park for a well-rounded session. The modern architecture of Merage, the contemporary water feature, and the natural park setting give your gallery three distinct visual styles. Most business graduates also want the classic Peter the Anteater statue shot, which is a short walk from the business school area.
