UCI School of Law Graduation Photos

The UCI School of Law has quickly become one of the most respected law schools in California since its founding. Graduating with your JD is a monumental achievement, and your graduation photos should convey the professionalism, determination, and pride that define your law school journey.

Law school graduates often have distinctive regalia, including a purple hood or stole that signifies the Juris Doctor degree. This pop of color photographs beautifully against the green backdrop of Aldrich Park or the stone facade of Langson Library. I recommend wearing your full academic regalia for at least part of the session, then switching to a professional outfit for a second look.

The Langson Library steps are a popular choice for law graduates because the classical columns and formal architecture complement the gravity of a law degree. I also recommend the MSTB arches for their European feel and Aldrich Park for a more relaxed, natural setting.

Many law graduates bring their bar prep books, a gavel, or other legal props for fun, personalized shots. These details add character to your gallery and give you images that celebrate both the academic rigor and the personality behind the degree.
