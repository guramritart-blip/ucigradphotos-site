Campus Locations

Best Photo Spots at UCI

UC Irvine offers a wide variety of scenic backdrops for graduation portraits. Here are six of the most popular locations I recommend for your session.

Aldrich Park

The heart of campus. Towering trees, lush greenery, and dappled sunlight make Aldrich Park the most iconic UCI photo location. The winding paths create depth in every frame, and the natural shade keeps the light soft even on bright days.

Engineering Gateway

The fountain and modern architecture of the Engineering Gateway provide a clean, contemporary backdrop. This spot is especially popular with STEM graduates who want to celebrate their achievement right outside the building where they spent countless hours studying.

Langson Library

With its striking exterior and grand columns, Langson Library gives your photos a classic, timeless feel. The steps leading up to the entrance create natural leading lines that draw the eye directly to you in your cap and gown.

Humanities Gateway

The Humanities Gateway building offers textured walls and covered walkways that provide beautiful, even light. It is a less crowded alternative to Aldrich Park, making it perfect for graduates who want a quieter, more intimate setting for their portraits.

Ring Road

The circular pathway that connects the entire campus is lined with trees and open spaces. Ring Road is ideal for walking shots, candid moments, and group photos. Its varied scenery means every few steps offer a new backdrop.

Peter the Anteater

No UCI graduation photo session is complete without a shot with Peter the Anteater. The bronze mascot statue near the Student Center is a beloved campus landmark that instantly tells the world where you earned your degree.
