About Me

Hey, I'm G

My name is Guramrit Malhotra, or just to keep it simple, "G". I have been photographing graduates since 2019 and very much enjoy it more each year. I myself am an alumni from UCI (class of 2021, but hey, finished in 2023, shoutout COVID) which kind of makes sense?? UCI is the very place where my business as well as creative pathway began.

I moved to the states for my undergrad in 2017 but life had different plans and now I get to do what I never thought I would be able to, and will not trade places with nobody.

I also do wedding photography and am currently working on opening my own studio, which has been fun and interesting. If you'd like to check out my wedding work as well, here is the website: bokehweddings.com

Being a photographer and filmmaker is a huge part of who I am but I also enjoy just meeting new people. Every grad has their own fair share of traumas haha, and I like to curate the photoshoot as a bit of a hangout and have some conversations of substance (cmon, it's like 2 hours, how much small talk will we do?). But hey, if you have booked with me or are deciding to, congratulations! It's no easy task to graduate but you did it. You should be proud :,)
